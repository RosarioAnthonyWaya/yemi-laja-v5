About the Event

When the World is Loud, We Gather.

Worship in Chaos is more than a concert. It's a live worship experience that creates space for stillness, encounter, and transformation in the middle of an increasingly noisy world.

Yemi leads the room through music, spoken word, and moments of raw communal prayer — creating an atmosphere that cuts through distraction and meets people exactly where they are.
